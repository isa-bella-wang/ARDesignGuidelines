AR Design Guidelines

These guidelines contain research-backed principles and best practices that help you design clear, comfortable, and usable experiences for spatial computing.

As you design interfaces for augmented reality, keep these principles in mind:

Physiological Safety

Design within visual system limits by placing content at stable depths, respecting field-of-view boundaries, and balancing brightness and transparency

Cognitive Clarity

Design within attention and processing limits by prioritizing essential information, reducing visual competition, and revealing detail gradually

Physical Ergonomics

Place interfaces and interaction targets within comfortable reach and viewing zones to prevent fatigue, strain, and unintended motion
